# Billing

Alora Eat uses a simple flat fee billing model.

## Free tier

The free tier is **free forever**. No credit card needed.

Includes:
- QR code ordering
- Kitchen board
- Waiter management
- Menu management
- Multi-branch support
- Staff management
- Inventory per branch

---

## How billing works

Billing starts when you create your **first branch**.

- Every 30 days an invoice is generated
- You have **7 days** to pay after the invoice is generated
- If unpaid after 7 days your account is **suspended**
- If still unpaid after 4 more days your account is **expired**
- Paying any invoice immediately restores full access

---

## Paying an invoice

Payment links are only shown in your **Dashboard** — never in emails.

1. Log in as Super Admin
2. Go to **Dashboard**
3. Find the invoice under Billing
4. Click **Pay Now**
5. Complete payment via Paystack

---

## Account statuses

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Full access |
| **Suspended** | Login works, orders blocked |
| **Expired** | Fully blocked |
